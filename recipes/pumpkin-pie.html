Mary's Pumpkin Pie

Description

Ingredients

Crust

  • 1 cup + 2 Tbsp flour
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/3 cup of canola oil
  • 2 Tbsp ice water

Filling

  • 1 sugar pumpkin
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 cup packed light brown sugar
  • 1 Tbsp all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 2 1/2 teaspoons

Pumpkin Pie Spice

  • 3 Tbsp ground cinnamon
  • 2 tsp ground ginger
  • 2 tsp nutmeg
  • 1 1/2 tsp ground allspice
  • 1 1/2 tsp ground cloves
