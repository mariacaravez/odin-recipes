Pumpkin Pie Spice

Description

Use pumpkin pie spice to flavor your favorite fall pies, muffins, cookies, and cakes. You can also use it to spice up your whipped cream, yogurt, lattes, oatmeal, or roasted fall veggies. It is a great spice to have on hand during the chilly fall months.

Ingredients

  • 3 Tbsp ground cinnamon
  • 2 Tsp ground ginger
  • 2 Tsp nutmeg
  • 1 1/2 Tsp ground allspice
  • 1 1/2 Tsp ground cloves

Steps

  1. In a small bowl, whisk together cinnamon, ginger, nutmeg, allspice, and cloves until well combined. Store in a small jar or container.
